The things you don't want to miss

Upark-Festival

When: 16-18 Julu 2020

Where: Kiev, Ukraine

Tickets: 100$

The UPark Festival is a major music event and festival of a new format for Ukraine that stands with the best European counterparts and even competes with many of them.

Tomorrowland

When: 14-21 March 2020

Where: Alpe d'Huez, France

Tickets: 150$

Tomorrowland is the world's biggest dance music festival, bringing an A-list lineup of DJs from across the dance music spectrum to Boom in Belgium each summer.

Coachella

When: 10-12 April 2020 & 17-19 April 2020

Where: Empire Polo Club, Indio CA, United States of America

Tickets: 195$

Coachella is a massive zeitgeist-chasing music festival in Indio, California. The biggest musical celebration in the world, the festival has to be on any music fan's bucket list.
